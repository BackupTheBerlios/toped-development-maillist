Hi Graham,

I had a look. The CIF file is fine. There is a problem with the 
conversion here. I don't want to get into details, but the bottom line 
is that I'm loosing precision during the CIF import and in the general 
case it might result in imprecise or even loss of data which would be a 
very serious flaw. It's fixable of course, but I'll need a couple of 
days on this. The assert will disappear and the crash with it, but it 
was placed there to catch this kind of problems.

One can argue of course that 15nm is out of the CIF precision, but 
that's certainly not the point. The point is the precision of the target 
DB (TDT) and it certainly takes this kind of sizes.

Thanks for the report. I'll keep you posted ...
